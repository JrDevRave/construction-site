About Built Right

35 years. One standard. No shortcuts.

[Photo: Founder / team on site]

Started With a Hammer, Built a Reputation

Built Right Construction didn't start with a business plan. It started with a young carpenter who learned the trade from his father, picked up every skill he could, and spent the next 35 years proving that construction can be done honestly.

That means showing up when we say we will. Giving you a price and sticking to it. Building things that hold up — not just things that pass inspection. Every wall we frame, every tile we set, every foundation we pour gets the same attention whether it's a small bathroom or a full addition.

We're not the cheapest. We're not the flashiest. We're the crew your neighbor recommends because we did right by them.

How We Work

These aren't slogans. They're how we've operated for three decades.

One Crew, Start to Finish

Your project doesn't get passed between random subcontractors. The crew that starts your job finishes it. You know who's in your home and who's responsible for the work.

Straight Talk, Honest Pricing

We tell you what it costs before we start. If something changes, we talk about it first. No surprise invoices, no hidden fees, no "we found something" upsells.

Demo and Rebuild Under One Roof

Most companies do one or the other. We do both. Tear it out, haul it off, and build it back — all coordinated by one team. Fewer handoffs means fewer problems.

Clean Sites, Clear Communication

We clean up every day before we leave. We answer calls and texts. If there's a delay, you hear it from us before you notice it yourself.

Built to Code, Built to Last

We don't build to the minimum. We build to the standard we'd want in our own homes. Proper materials, proper technique, proper inspections.

Local and Accountable

We live and work in this community. Our reputation is our business. If something isn't right, we come back and make it right — that's not a policy, it's personal.
