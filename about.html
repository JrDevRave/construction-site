About

Company Story

A contractor homeowners can talk to without the sales pitch

Dino's Construction and Demolition LLC is meant to feel local because it is local. The company focuses on homeowners in Macomb County and St. Clair County who want practical guidance, honest work, and a contractor that can handle the dirty phase and the finished phase of a project.

That family-owned feel shows up in how jobs are discussed, how expectations are set, and how much the finished work matters. The goal is simple: show up, communicate clearly, and leave the homeowner with something solid and well done.

Whether the job starts with full demolition, a remodel, or a custom build idea, the approach stays the same: be straightforward, do quality work, and treat the home with respect.

  • Preferred service area is Macomb County and St. Clair County.
  • Projects can include remodels, additions, garage work, demolition, and custom requests.
  • Honest conversations and dependable workmanship stay at the center of the process.
