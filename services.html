Services

Construction, remodeling, and demolition services

Dino's Construction and Demolition LLC works across the projects homeowners call about most, with room for one-off custom scopes too.

What We Do

From demo to done — here's what we handle

Whether you're starting fresh or finishing something already in progress, Dino's Construction and Demolition LLC handles all phases of the job for homeowners across Macomb County and St. Clair County.

Kitchen Remodels

Kitchen Remodels

Ready to stop working around a kitchen that doesn't fit how you actually cook? We tear out what isn't working and rebuild it the right way — better layout, better storage, and finishes that hold up.

  • Cabinets, countertops, flooring, and trim
  • Layout changes after demo — not just a surface refresh
  • Islands, updated lighting placement, and flow improvements
Bathroom Remodels

Bathroom Remodels

A bathroom remodel done right goes past new tile. We handle the full scope — from tearing out the old work to installing finishes that look clean and stay that way.

  • Showers, tubs, vanities, flooring, and fixtures
  • Full tear-outs when the space needs a complete do-over
  • Guest baths, master baths, and half-bath updates
Basement Finishing

Basement Finishing

Your basement doesn't have to be a storage dump. We frame it out, finish it properly, and turn it into real living space — a family room, a rec room, a home office, whatever you need.

  • Framing, drywall, flooring, and finish work
  • Egress planning, lighting, and room layout
  • Adds usable square footage without adding on to the house
Room Additions

Room Additions

When you've outgrown the floor plan, an addition is the answer. We build it out so the new space feels like it was always part of the home — not tacked on.

  • Extra bedrooms, sunrooms, family rooms, and flex spaces
  • Structural and framing work tied into the existing home
  • Interior finishes matched to blend with what's already there
Garage Builds

Garage Builds

No garage, or one that doesn't fit what you need? We build detached and attached garages from the ground up — practical, solid, and built to match your property.

  • Detached or attached builds, single or multi-car
  • Shell framing, siding, roofing, and doors
  • Workshop space, extra storage, or just a place to park
Full Demolition

Full Demolition

Sometimes you need to clear it all out before anything good can happen. We handle the demo phase — cleanly, safely, and ready for whatever comes next.

  • Interior room tear-outs and full structural demo
  • Safe removal of walls, flooring, fixtures, and framing
  • Site left clean and ready for the next phase of work
Custom Projects

Custom Projects

Not everything fits into a neat category, and that's fine. If you have a project idea that's a little different — or a scope that mixes several types of work — reach out and we'll talk it through.

  • Mixed-scope jobs that combine demo, build, and finish
  • One-off builds, unique spaces, and specialty requests
  • Describe what you're trying to do — we'll tell you if we can help
